Overview
I’m a product designer / graphic designer / brand designer / headshot photographer / website strategist (a.k.a. Creative Specialized Generalist) with over 10 years of agency experience.
‍
I listen to business goals to create user-focused and client-minded designs. Why? Because consistent & visually strong branding helps to keep audiences listening—so my clients can do what they do best without interference.
